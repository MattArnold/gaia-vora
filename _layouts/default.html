Gaia Vora

an unpublished board game by Nemo Rathwald, designer of "Overworld"

1 to 4 players

40 to 60 minutes

Ages 12 and up

Fill the desolate planet of Gaia with verdant life. Arrange your carnivores, herbivores, and plants, to eat your opponents, and survive on the highest stacks of land tiles. But beware Vora, the Feastmoon!
